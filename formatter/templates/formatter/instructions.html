Step-by-Step Guide

How to Use the Tool

Follow these simple steps to format and export your AI-generated content:

1

Paste Your Text

Copy text from ChatGPT, Claude, or any AI tool and paste it into the text area. Our tool supports markdown formatting including:

  • Headings: # H1, ## H2, ### H3
  • Bold: **bold text**
  • Italic: *italic text*
  • Lists: Numbered and bullet points
  • Code: Inline `code` and code blocks
  • Tables: Markdown tables
2

Click Format

Hit the "‚ú® Format" button to process your text. The tool will convert markdown to formatted HTML and display a live preview.

3

Review Preview

Check the formatted preview to ensure everything looks good. The preview shows exactly how your document will appear.

4

Export Your Document

Choose your preferred format:

Enter a filename when prompted, and your document will download automatically.

5

Access History

All formatted content is automatically saved in the history sidebar. You can:

  • Load any previous format with one click
  • Search through your history
  • Delete individual items or clear all history
  • Re-export any saved content
